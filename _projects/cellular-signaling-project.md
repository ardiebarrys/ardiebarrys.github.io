This personal project develops a conceptual framework for interpreting cellular signaling pathways as dynamic regulatory circuits.

The work includes ideas related to redox signal resolution, environmental sensing, immune regulation, temporal signal encoding, phase-separated regulatory states, and failures of signal resolution in disease.

Related papers include the NRF2-KEAP1 and CYP1A1 manuscripts.
